Chief Guest

Prof. Camilla Baratto

Senior Researcher with the National Research Council-National Institute of Optics (CNR-INO), Brescia, Italy & Topical Editor of IEEE Sensor Journal

http://sensor.unibs.it/people/dr-camilla-baratto

Prof. Camilla Baratto has published 70 articles in international journals with referee. She is coauthor of more than 100 presentations to national and international conferences; she was invited and presented four invited communications and one keynote presentation. Her research interests include conductometric and optically addressable gas sensors, continuous wave photoluminescence and Raman spectroscopy, preparation and characterization of gas sensors of nanostructured metal oxides, characterization of metal oxides (1D) nanowires and nanostructures, and characterization of 2D materials. Her H-index is 27 (source ISI).
